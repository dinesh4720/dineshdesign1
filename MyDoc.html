Design process

Problems from previous consultation apps

  • Complicated Appointment booking page.
  • Adding payment options.
  • Emergency Services.
  • Viewing past appointments & prescription downloading.
  • Appointment remainder and schedule remainder.

Information Architecture

User Flow

Solutions

  • Seperate scrollable timings with date and month selector.
  • Adding new card for payment made easy with saved info.
  • Emergency Services a new section in the homepage.
  • Seperate section for viewing upcomming, past appointments.
  • Appointment remainder and schedule remainder in the homepage.

Reflection

Designing an end to end experience with all the necessary applications was a huge challenge in this project. Designing for multiple form factors at once made me a fast paced person. Got to learn the importance of visual design and visual hierarchy and how it makes the users more engaging. Although I ideated on multiple solutions, all I could build was a minimum viable product yet a useful one.
